Scenario: Title: Parent interest in Education Quality Narrative: As a parent I would like to find out about the quality of the education at UL so that I can decide whether I want my child to go there. Acceptance Criteria: Scenario1: Desktop website - Virtual Open Day

Given as a parent
Given that I am on the Virtual Open Day desktop site
When I click on the Parents link
Then a page with links tailored to Parents concerns is displayed
Scenario1: Academic Outcomes
Given that I am on the Virtual Open Day - Parents page
And I need information on Academic outcomes for undergraduates
When I click on Study At UL
And click on Academic
Then I am brought to a page with links to details of Academic outcomes
Scenario2: Student Supports
Given that I am on the Virtual Open Day - Parents page
And I need information on Student Supports for undergraduates
When I click on Student Supports
Then I am brought to a page with details of student supports, with an emphasis on the First Seven Weeks initiative.
Scenario3: Download prospectus
Given that I am on the Virtual Open Day - Parents page
And I want to download the UL Prospectus
When I click "Download Prospectus" button
Then the latest version of the Prospectus PDF is downloaded
Scenario4: Availability of accommodation
Given that I am on the Virtual Open Day - Parents page
And I need information on accommodation for first years
When I click on "On Campus Accommodation" link
Then I am brought to a page with links to details of on site accommodation
And this will include details of how to apply, costs and conditions to qualify for on campus accommodation
