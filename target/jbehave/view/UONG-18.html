Scenario: Title: Law Student open day schedule Narrative: As a prospective Law student I want an agenda of the times and locations of different activities and talks so I can plan my day at UL effectively Acceptance Criteria: Scenario 1: Using event buddy to get user preferences

Given as a prospective Law student
Given that I have logged in to the mobile app
When I click on the "Event Buddy" option from the home page
And specify that I am interested in Law
Then I will get a schedule with all the events and meetings to do with Law
And I can filter which events I want to keep in my schedule
Scenario 2: Mobile App "What's On" feature
Given that I have logged in to the mobile app
And my schedule has been generated using the Event Buddy
When I click on the "Whats On" feature from the home screen
Then I will be able to choose which Law talks and events I want to go to
Scenario 3: Refer to "My Schedule" throughout the day
Given that I have logged in to the mobile app
When I click on the "My Schedule" option from the home page
And my schedule is displayed
Then I can refer to this to see which event is next
